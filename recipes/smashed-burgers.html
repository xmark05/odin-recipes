Smashed Burgers Recipe

Description

Smashed Burgers are a simple yet delicious way to enjoy a classic burger. Ground beef is pressed thinly onto a hot skillet, creating a crispy, caramelized crust while staying juicy inside. Topped with melted cheese and served on a toasted bun with your favorite toppings, these burgers are quick to make and packed with flavor.

Recipe:

    450g (1 lb) ground beef (preferably 80% lean, 20% fat) Salt and black pepper, to taste 4 slices of cheddar cheese (or your favorite cheese) 4 burger buns, lightly toasted 1 tablespoon butter or oil (for toasting the buns) Toppings (optional): sliced pickles, lettuce, sliced tomato, thinly sliced onion Condiments: ketchup, mustard, mayonnaise

GUIDE

  1. Divide and Roll

    2. Divide 450g (1 lb) of ground beef into 4 loose balls.

  2. Preheat Skillet

    3. Heat a cast-iron skillet on high until very hot.

    Smashed and Season

    Place the beef balls in the skillet, smash them flat with a spatula until about 1/4 inch thick. Season with salt and pepper.

  3. Cook and Flip

    4. Cook for 2–3 minutes until crispy. Flip, add a slice of cheese, and cook for another 1–2 minutes.

  4. Toast Buns

    5. Toast burger buns in a separate pan with butter until golden brown.

  5. Assemble

    6. Stack patties on buns, add toppings and condiments, and enjoy!
