Lasagne Recipe

Description

This delicious homemade lasagna is a classic Italian comfort dish that layers tender sheets of pasta with a rich and hearty meat sauce, creamy ricotta, and a generous amount of gooey melted mozzarella cheese. The recipe starts with a savory, slow-cooked sauce made with ground beef, tomatoes, onions, garlic, and a blend of Italian herbs. Between the layers of sauce and pasta, a creamy mixture of ricotta, Parmesan, and fresh parsley adds a luscious texture and flavor. Finished with a golden, bubbly cheese topping, this lasagna is baked to perfection, creating a comforting and satisfying meal that's perfect for family gatherings, special occasions, or a cozy weeknight dinner.

Recipe:

    1 pound ground beef

    1 pound bulk Italian sausage

    1/2 cup warm water

    1 cup sliced black olives

    1 pinch basil

    2 teaspoons garlic powder

    1 tablespoon dried oregano

    1 1/2 teaspoons dried sage

    2 teaspoons ground black pepper

    2 tablespoons minced garlic

    1 tablespoon dried onion flakes

    2 (14 ounce) jars marinara sauce

    1 (16 ounce) package lasagna noodles

    8 ounces ricotta cheese

    1 pound mozzarella cheese

Guide

  1. Make the Meat Sauce:

    2. Sauté 1 chopped onion and 3 minced garlic cloves in olive oil.

    Add 500g (1 lb) ground beef and brown.

    Stir in a 800g (28 oz) can of crushed tomatoes, 2 tbsp tomato paste, and Italian herbs (basil, oregano).

    Simmer for 20 minutes, adding salt and pepper to taste.

  2. Cook the Noodles:

    3. Boil 12 lasagna noodles until al dente. Drain and set aside.

  3. Prepare the Cheese Mixture:

    4. Mix 500g (2 cups) ricotta with 1 egg, 1/2 cup grated Parmesan, salt, and fresh parsley.

  4. Assemble:

    5. In a 9x13-inch dish, layer: meat sauce, noodles, ricotta mix, and shredded mozzarella. Repeat 3 times, ending with mozzarella on top.

  5. Bake:

    6. Cover with foil and bake at 190°C (375°F) for 25 minutes. Uncover and bake another 25 minutes until bubbly and golden.

  6. Rest and Serve:

    7. Let it sit for 10 minutes before slicing. Enjoy hot!
