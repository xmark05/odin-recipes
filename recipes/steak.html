avocado, steak, and fried eggs recipe

Description

This hearty and flavorful dish brings together tender slices of perfectly grilled steak, creamy avocado, and a golden, sunny-side-up fried egg. The steak is seasoned with a blend of spices and seared to perfection, creating a juicy and savory base. Itâ€™s paired with fresh avocado, which adds a creamy texture and mild, nutty flavor. The crowning glory is a runny fried egg, with its rich yolk cascading over the steak and avocado, adding a delicious, velvety finish. Perfect for breakfast, brunch, or a protein-packed dinner, this dish is as satisfying as it is delicious.

Recipe:

    1 ripe avocado, halved and sliced

    200g (7 oz) steak (like ribeye or sirloin)

    1 tablespoon olive oil or butter

    Salt and black pepper, to taste

    1/2 teaspoon garlic powder

    1/2 teaspoon smoked paprika (optional)

    2 large eggs

    Fresh parsley or chives for garnish (optional)

Guide:

  1. Cook the Steak:

    2. Season a 200g(7oz) steak with salt and pepper

    Heat a skillet over medium-high heat with a bit of olive oil

    Cook steak for 3-4 minutes per side (for medium.rare) or until desired doneness.Let it rest, then slice thinly.

  2. Fry the Eggs

    3. In the same skillet, add a little butter

    Crack 2 eggs into the pan and cook until the whites are set and yolks are still runny (or to your preference).

  3. Prepare the Avocado:

    4. Slice 1 ripe avocado and squeeze a bit of lemon juice over it to prevent browning.

  4. Assemble:

    5. Lay avocado slices on a plate, top with steak slices, and add the fried eggs.

    Garnish with salt, pepper, and fresh herbs(like parsley or chives)
